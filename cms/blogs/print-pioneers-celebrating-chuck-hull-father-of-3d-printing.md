Chuck Hull's journey began in the early 1980s when he invented stereolithography, the first commercial 3D printing technology. His innovation laid the foundation for the entire industry, leading to his co-founding of 3D Systems. ### **Global Impact and Legacy**

Today, Chuck Hull's invention is not just a tool but a transformative force in manufacturing, medicine, and beyond. His work has earned him a spot in the National Inventors Hall of Fame, celebrating hislasting impact on the world.
